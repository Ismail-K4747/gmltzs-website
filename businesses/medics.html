GM Medics Pharmacy

About This Business

Your Trusted Pharmacy Partner in Tanzania

GM Medics Pharmacy is committed to improving healthcare outcomes across Dar es Salaam by providing reliable access to quality prescription and over-the-counter medications. We serve individual patients, families, and the local community with a comprehensive range of medicines and health products.

Our licensed pharmacists, rigorous quality assurance processes, and established supplier relationships ensure that every medication we dispense meets TFDA standards — safely, affordably, and with professional care.
