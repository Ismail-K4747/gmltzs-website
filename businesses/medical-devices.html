About This Business

Equipping Healthcare with the Right Tools

General Malick Limited: Medical Devices & Lab Equipment is dedicated to sourcing and supplying high-quality medical devices, diagnostic instruments, and laboratory equipment from trusted global manufacturers. We support hospitals, clinics, laboratories, and research institutions across Tanzania.

From advanced imaging systems and surgical instruments to laboratory analysers and consumables, our experienced team provides end-to-end support — from product selection and procurement to installation, training, and after-sales maintenance.
