GML Cargo

About This Business

Seamless Logistics, Delivered on Time

GML Cargo is your trusted partner for freight forwarding and logistics services in East Africa. Operating from Dar es Salaam — Tanzania's busiest port city — we handle import and export cargo with efficiency, transparency, and competitive pricing.

From customs clearance at Dar es Salaam Port to warehousing, inland transport, and last-mile delivery, our experienced team manages the entire supply chain so you can focus on growing your business. We handle cargo from Dubai, China, India, Europe, and across Africa.

🚢

Freight Forwarding

Sea freight and air freight forwarding services for imports and exports via Dar es Salaam.

📋

Customs Clearance

Expert customs brokerage services ensuring smooth, compliant clearance of goods at all Tanzanian ports.

🏭

Warehousing & Storage

Secure, well-managed warehousing facilities for short-term and long-term cargo storage in Dar es Salaam.

🚛

Last-Mile Delivery

Reliable inland transportation and last-mile delivery across Tanzania and neighbouring countries.

🌍

International Shipping

End-to-end shipping coordination from global origins including Dubai, China, India, and Europe.

📊

Supply Chain Consulting

Advisory services to optimise your logistics operations, reduce costs, and improve delivery timelines.

Whether you're importing, exporting, or need warehousing in Dar es Salaam, our team is ready to handle your cargo with care and efficiency.
