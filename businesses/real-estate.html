About This Business

Building the Future of Tanzanian Real Estate

GML Real Estate is at the forefront of property development in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We specialise in creating high-quality residential and commercial spaces that meet the growing demands of a dynamic East African market.

From prime land acquisition to architectural design, construction management, and property sales, our team delivers end-to-end solutions tailored to investors, homebuyers, and businesses seeking premium locations in Tanzania's commercial capital.

Residential Developments

Modern apartments, townhouses, and standalone homes designed for comfort and value appreciation.

Commercial Properties

Office spaces, retail outlets, and mixed-use developments in prime Dar es Salaam locations.

Property Management

Full-service management including tenant relations, maintenance, and rent collection for property owners.

Land Sales & Advisory

Expert guidance on land acquisition, due diligence, title verification, and investment advisory.

Architecture & Design

Architectural planning and interior design services that blend modern aesthetics with functional living.

Investment Advisory

Market analysis and advisory services for local and international real estate investors in Tanzania.
