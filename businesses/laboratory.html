About This Business

Precision Diagnostics You Can Trust

GM Labs & Diagnostic Point provides a full range of clinical and diagnostic laboratory services operated by experienced technicians and pathologists. Our laboratory is equipped with modern analytical instruments, ensuring high accuracy and fast turnaround times for all tests.

We serve individual patients, hospitals, clinics, and corporate clients with everything from routine blood work and health screenings to specialised pathology and microbiological testing — all in compliance with Tanzanian regulatory standards.

Clinical Diagnostics

Comprehensive blood work, urinalysis, and biochemistry testing using modern automated analysers.

Pathology Services

Histopathology and cytology analysis by qualified pathologists for accurate disease diagnosis.

Health Screening Packages

Comprehensive wellness checkup packages for individuals, families, and corporate groups.

Corporate Wellness Testing

On-site and facility-based health assessments for businesses and their employees.

Microbiology Testing

Culture, sensitivity, and infectious disease testing to support accurate treatment decisions.

Digital Results Delivery

Secure online access to test results, enabling patients and providers to receive reports quickly.
