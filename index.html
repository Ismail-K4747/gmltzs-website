Who We Are

Building Legacies,
One Business at a Time

General Malick Limited (GML) is a family of ambitious, purpose-driven businesses united by a shared commitment to quality, integrity, and lasting impact across East Africa.

Headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, we operate across four key sectors — real estate development, medical and pharmaceutical services, diagnostic laboratory solutions, and cargo logistics — bringing the same relentless focus on delivering value to every client we serve.

Whether you are looking for prime properties in Dar es Salaam, trusted medical care and pharmaceuticals, reliable laboratory diagnostics, or efficient cargo and logistics solutions, General Malick Limited has the expertise and the passion to exceed your expectations.
